APP NAME
Auto Loan Calculator — UK

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max)
HP & PCP car finance calculator with VED road tax, amortisation schedule & PDF.

FULL DESCRIPTION
Plan your next car finance deal with confidence. Auto Loan Calculator UK handles both standard Hire Purchase (HP) and Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) — the way British borrowers actually buy cars.

TOOLS INCLUDED
• Payment Calculator — monthly or fortnightly payment for HP and PCP
• Amortisation Schedule — full month-by-month breakdown of capital vs. interest
• Early Payoff — calculate the benefit of overpayments under FCA CONC rules
• Loan Comparator — compare two finance deals side by side
• Trade-In Estimator — subtract your part-exchange value before financing
• History & PDF Export — save unlimited calculations and export summary PDFs

UK-SPECIFIC FEATURES
• PCP mode — enter your GMFV (guaranteed minimum future value / balloon payment) as a percentage of vehicle price
• Road Tax (VED) — built-in 2024/25 annual rates for Electric, Petrol <1000cc, Petrol >1000cc, Diesel/Hybrid, and custom entry
• Representative APR display with FCA CONC compliance note
• DVLA-compliant VED annual rates shown in the calculator
• VAT-inclusive vehicle price input
