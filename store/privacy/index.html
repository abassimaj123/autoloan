1. Overview

AutoLoan is a financial calculator app. All calculations are performed entirely on your device. We are strongly committed to your privacy: we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

AutoLoan does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Financial account numbers, credit card numbers, or banking details
  • Vehicle identification numbers or purchase records
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Device contacts, photos, or files

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

AutoLoan stores your saved calculations (loan parameters and results) in your device's local storage using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third party. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob (free users)

The free tier displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (GAID / IDFA) and limited device information to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users see no ads and AdMob is not loaded for them.

4.2 Google Play In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We receive only a confirmation token — no payment card details or billing information are transmitted to us.

4.3 Firebase Analytics (optional)

We may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised, aggregated usage events (e.g., "calculator used", "paywall shown"). These events contain no personally identifiable information. You can opt out of analytics data collection in the app's Settings screen.

5. In-App Review

After several uses, the app may ask you to rate it via the Google Play or Apple in-app review prompt. Responding is entirely voluntary. No review text or rating is accessible to us through this prompt.

6. California Residents — CCPA Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected — we collect none
  • Request deletion of personal information — nothing to delete on our servers
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell data
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise these rights or ask questions, contact: abassimaj@gmail.com

7. Canadian Residents — PIPEDA

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to access and correct any personal information we hold about you. Because AutoLoan does not collect personal information, there is nothing to access or correct on our end. Local app data remains solely under your control on your device.

For privacy inquiries, contact: abassimaj@gmail.com

8. UK & EU Residents — UK GDPR

AutoLoan does not process personal data as defined under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) or the EU GDPR. Anonymised analytics events do not constitute personal data under these frameworks.

If you believe we have processed your personal data and wish to exercise your rights (access, erasure, portability, objection), contact: abassimaj@gmail.com

9. Children's Privacy

AutoLoan is not directed at children under 13 (US) or under 16 (UK/EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Data Retention

No personal data is retained on our servers because none is collected. Local device data (saved calculations) is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Material changes will be announced in the app's release notes. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
